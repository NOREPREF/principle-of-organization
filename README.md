## Abstract

We propose the **Principle of Organization**: across all scales of nature, enduring macroscopic structures—from atomic nuclei to living organisms and engineered fusion systems—emerge and persist as dissipative structures. These are sustained by continuous energy gradients generated by one or more fundamental forces, with entropy production exported to the environment.

This work extends Ilya Prigogine’s framework of non-equilibrium thermodynamics, unifying stellar nucleosynthesis, pulsed magnetic compression fusion, and biological self-assembly under a single coherent principle.
